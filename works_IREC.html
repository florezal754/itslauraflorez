Data product development

Python-based software to generate hourly average and marginal factors for building assessment in estimating CO2 emissions (CE) and non-renewable primary energy (PE) of the grid electricity. Web application retrieves the data from open data sources and is planned to be the back-end of a web application to show historical and forecasted data. Technologies: Python, Flask, Jupyter Notebooks, Git, Heroku, JavaScript, Trello.
