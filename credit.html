Recognitions and/or interest of work

Cultural Initiatives and art residency at Fabra i Coats (Barcelona, 2023). Her entrepreneurship and project related to the cultural and creative sector has been selected by the Commission of project selection of the open call “Cultural initiatives” to attend the fabric of creation.

Selection to develop innovative entrepreneurship projects at Tecnocampus (Tecnocampus Mataró, 2023). She was selected to participate in a programme to research, initiate, plan and launch innovative proposals working with technology and social impact.

4FYN startups 8.1 stage at the MWC 2023 (Barcelona, 2023). Her UI/UX proposal was selected as winner of the contest "Le Wagon UX/UI projects".

Art residency for NES artists in residence at Skagaströnd (Iceland, 2022). She has shown her work in a group exhibition at the end of her NES residency in Skagaströnd (Iceland, 2022). The work she developed questioned if research could be art, the NFT speculation in art practice, the role of decentralized and emerging technologies in art, the meaning of art for artists, and their perspectives on the meaning of artistic practice in society.

Selection for the development of an artistic project MUSAC (León, 2020). She was selected to participate in a short programme to develop a project with recognized artist.
