Career path

In 2018 I graduated from a Bachelor's in Social Sciences where I felt in love with data, applied statistics and consumer resesarch.
In 2019 I graduated from a Master's in Design for Emergent Futures and completed my thesis with a design prototype; mainly making use of data, IoT technologies and digital fabrication tools.
After that, I understood that I needed to go deeper in full stack web technologies as a way to escalate projects I was working on. I completed technical courses in full stack and AI and ML.
In 2021, I worked as a research engineer at IREC where I developed a data product, a webservice showcasing marginal signals the CO2 electricity consumption and published two publications.
Recently in 2023, I create a mobile app based business, performing UX research, UX/UI design and development.
